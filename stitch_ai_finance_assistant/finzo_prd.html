# Finzo - Product Requirements Document ## Project Overview Finzo is a comprehensive personal finance tracking application designed for the Bangladeshi market, featuring a smart AI assistant that understands English, Bengali, and Banglish. The app focuses on seamless transaction entry through natural language and a premium "Glassmorphism" visual style. ## Core Features ### 1. AI Core System (The "Heart" of Finzo) - **Multi-lingual AI:** Supports English, Bengali, and Banglish (Bengali in English script). - **Auto-Transaction System:** Converts natural language (e.g., "50 taka cha") into structured data and saves it instantly. - **Voice System:** Supports voice-to-text input and voice replies for a hands-free experience. - **Financial Monitoring:** Analyzes spending patterns and provides smart suggestions. ### 2. Financial Management - **Transaction Types:** Expense, Income, Cash Out, Deposit, Transfer. - **Source Integration:** Supports bKash, Nagad, Rocket, Upay, CellFin, and major Bangladeshi banks (Islami Bank, UCBL, etc.) with official logos. - **Advanced Dashboard:** Real-time balances, daily/monthly summaries, and interactive charts. - **Budgeting & Categories:** Flexible category management and monthly budget tracking with alerts. - **Daily Habit Tracking:** Specialized tracking for common habits like tea, cigarettes, and water. ### 3. Smart Utilities - **Advanced Calculator:** Full mathematical functions with memory and transaction history integration. - **Smart Calendar:** Includes Bangladesh national holidays, Islamic holidays, and international events. - **Reports:** Detailed AI-driven insights and visual charts. ### 4. Technical & UI/UX - **UI Style:** Premium purple gradients, Glassmorphism, smooth animations, and micro-interactions. - **Themes:** Automated Light/Dark mode detection. - **Authentication:** Email, Phone (OTP), and Google Sign-In. - **Security:** JWT, Biometric (Fingerprint/FaceID), and PIN lock. - **Platform:** Responsive Web, PWA, and Android APK. ### 5. Administration - **Admin Panel:** For user management, system analytics, and overall control. ## Target Audience Individuals in Bangladesh looking for an effortless, tech-forward way to manage their money using their native language or Banglish.
